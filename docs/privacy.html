Privacy Policy

Alias Duel

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Alias Duel ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Alias Duel operates entirely on your device and does not transmit, store, or process any personal information on external servers.

Specifically, the App does not:

  • Collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Track your location
  • Access your camera, microphone, or photos
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Collect usage analytics or diagnostics
  • Contain third-party advertising or analytics SDKs

Data Storage

All game data (such as scores and settings) is stored locally on your device and is never shared with us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, the App is safe for users of all ages. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
