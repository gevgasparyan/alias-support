About the App

Alias Duel is a fast-paced, exciting word-guessing game designed to test your vocabulary and quick thinking. Gather your friends, split into teams, and see who can explain the most words before the timer runs out!

  • Multiple categories and difficulty levels
  • Customizable game rules and timers
  • Sleek, easy-to-use interface
  • Perfect for parties, family gatherings, and game nights
